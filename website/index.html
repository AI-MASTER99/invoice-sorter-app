Features

Everything you need
for customs processing

🤖
Dual AI verification
Every invoice is extracted twice independently. Results are cross-checked before export — no silent errors.
🧠
Product memory
The system learns your products. After the first invoice, known items are processed instantly with their tariff codes.
🔍
UK Tariff lookup
Search the official UK Trade Tariff directly from the app. Find commodity codes by product name in seconds.

Formatted Excel export
Download invoices as professionally formatted Excel files with totals, duty rates and VAT — ready for your broker.

Multi-user access
